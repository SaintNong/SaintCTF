What's a CTF?

If you are new to CTFs, then here's an explanation:

A CTF is a cybersecurity competition, where teams compete to solve as many challenges as they can, as fast as possible.

Challenges range across several categories, such as Crypto(graphy), reverse engineering, and web exploitation.
